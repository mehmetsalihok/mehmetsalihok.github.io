Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

At Codemso, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users who use the "Vakt-i Ezan" application. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is processed and protected while using our app.

1. Information Collection and Use

a. Location Information (GPS)

The Vakt-i Ezan application requires access to your device's precise or approximate location data to accurately calculate prayer times for your specific location and to indicate the Qibla direction.

  • This data is used instantaneously only when the application is active on your device or when the widget updates.
  • Your location data is never stored on our servers, tracked, or shared with third parties.
  • Calculations are performed entirely on your device (offline-first) or via trusted weather services instantaneously.

b. Notifications

Local notification services on your device are used to remind you of prayer times (Adhan or alerts). This process does not require an internet connection, and you can change your notification preferences at any time from the "Settings" menu.

2. Third-Party Services

We may use the following trusted third-party service providers to improve the quality and sustainability of our application:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements within the application. Google may collect anonymous data, such as the device Advertising ID, to personalize ads.
  • Google Firebase: May collect anonymous usage statistics to analyze crash reports (Crashlytics) and improve app performance.

3. Data Security

The security of your data is our priority. Your personal preferences (notification settings, selected city, etc.) are stored only in your device's memory and are not transmitted externally except through encrypted secure connections.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
